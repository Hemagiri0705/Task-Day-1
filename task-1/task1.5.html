Biriyani

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of Basmati Rice
  • 500g of Chicken
  • yogurt
  • onions(sliced)
  • Tomatoes(sliced)
  • Ginger Garlic Paste
  • Biriyani Masala
  • Oil
  • Salt&Chilli Powder
  • Coriander or Mint Leaves

    • Instructions

    Wash and soak Rice for atlest 20 or 30 mins

    Then half Cook it

    Marinate Chicken With Yogurt and Ginger Garlic Paste,for 1 hour

    Fry Onions and Tomatoes

    CooK Marinate Chicken until tender,add fried Onions and Tomatoes

    Again Cook the Rice with chicken.Top with Coriander Leaves

    Pour salt ,chilli powder required taste
